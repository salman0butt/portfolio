## Single Page vs Multi-Page Decision

**Verdict: KEEP SINGLE PAGE**

This single-page design is SUPERIOR for developer portfolios because:

✅ **HR screening**: Can review everything in 30 seconds of scrolling
✅ **Modern**: Shows you understand current UX trends
✅ **Mobile-friendly**: Easier on phones (where many recruiters browse)
✅ **Technical showcase**: Smooth animations prove React skills
✅ **SEO**: All content on one URL = better rankings
✅ **Load time**: Faster than navigating multiple pages

**Only go multi-page if:**
- You have 20+ major projects needing separate pages
- You're adding a full blog with 100+ articles
- You're building a personal brand site beyond just portfolio
